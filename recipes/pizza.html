Chicken Ranch Pizza

Description

the chicken ranch pizza is easy and tasteful pizza recipe that will leave you licking your fingers adn make your friends want to know the secret ingredinet added to the sauce to make them drool form the first bite

the chicken ranch pizza recipe is as easy as it can get,However you need to folow it strictly especially the SAUCE as an extra mg of salt could alter the taste dramarically "For example"

Ingredients

  • 1 can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 package Pizza Crust
  • 1 can Swanson® Premium White Chunk Chicken Breast in Water
  • 1 c. mozzarella cheese
  • 1 c. shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 8 slice bacon
  • Ranch Sauce
    • 1/2 c. mayonnaise
    • 1/2 c. sour cream
    • 3/4 c. buttermilk
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/4 c. freshly chopped parsley
    • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped dill
    • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped chives
    • 1 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
    • 1/2 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
    • 1/2 tsp. onion powder
    • Pinch cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spread pizza crust out onto a greased baking sheet.

  2. Step 2:

    Bake in the preheated oven until edges just start to brown, 7 to 8 minutes.

  3. Step 3:

    Combine sour cream and ranch mix in a bowl and spread onto the warm pizza crust. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Top with red onion, tomato, chicken, and bacon. Sprinkle with remaining cheese

  4. Step 4:

    Bake in the preheated oven until crust is browned to your liking and cheese is melted, 6 to 10 minutes more
